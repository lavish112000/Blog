# How to Clear Browser Cache to See New Blog Articles

If you are still seeing dummy articles (like "The AI Revolution" or "Neumorphism is Dead") instead of the new real articles, your browser is likely showing a cached version of the website.

Please follow these steps to clear your cache and see the latest content.

## Quick Fix (Hard Refresh)

For most browsers, you can force a reload of the page from the server:

- **Windows/Linux:** Press `Ctrl` + `F5` OR `Ctrl` + `Shift` + `R`
- **Mac:** Press `Cmd` + `Shift` + `R`

## Chrome / Edge

1. Click the three dots menu in the top right corner.
2. Go to **Settings** > **Privacy and security** > **Clear browsing data**.
3. Select **Cached images and files**.
4. Click **Clear data**.
5. Reload the website.

## Firefox

1. Click the hamburger menu (three lines) in the top right.
2. Go to **Settings** > **Privacy & Security**.
3. Scroll down to **Cookies and Site Data**.
4. Click **Clear Data...**.
5. Uncheck "Cookies and Site Data" (unless you want to clear logins too).
6. Check **Cached Web Content**.
7. Click **Clear**.

## Safari

1. Press `Option` + `Cmd` + `E` to empty the cache.
2. Reload the page.
